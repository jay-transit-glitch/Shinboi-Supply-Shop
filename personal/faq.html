Shipping & Delivery

How fast is "Urgent Summoning" delivery?

Urgent Summoning is our fastest relay service, guranteeing delivery to designated village supply depots within 12 hours of order confirmation, provided the order is placed during standard village business hours (0600-1800). This service utilzies Tier-1 tracking seals.

Do you ship outside of the Five Great Nations?

We primarily serve the major shinboi nations (Fire, Wind, Water, Earth, Lighting). However, we can arrange secure, discreet delivery to smaller nations or established client hideouts. Contact our logistics support team for a custom quote and security clearance.

Payment Methods

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept standard Village-issued credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), secure bank transfers (Fu-Jutsu Wire), and digital currency, including Amaterasu Coin.

What is the "Ninja Tax"?

The 5% Ninja Tax is a mandatory levy applied by the collective villages to fund mission casualty support and retirement programs for retired Kage. It is automatically calculated in your cart total.

Gear & Product Quality

Are you Kunai and Shuriken certified for mission use?

Yes. All of your primary weaponry (Kuani, Shuriken, Exploding Tags) are forged by certified Village smiths and tested by Jonin-level instructors. They meet or exceed official B-Rank mission quality standards.

What is the return policy on customized gear?

Customized uniforms or personalized weapons are non-refundable unless a manufacturing defect is present. Please verify all specifications (measurements, inscriptions, materials) before finalizing your custom order.

Returns & Exchanges

How do I start a return process?

\

To initiate a return, you must complete a formal Return Scroll Request form within 30 days of receipt. All items must be returned in their orginal, unused condition (unless defective) via a secure, tracked delivery seal.

What if my package is intercepted by rogue ninja?

All packages are insured aganist mission interference. If your tracking seal confirms interception or destruction by unauthorized parties, please file a report immediately. We will initiate a replacement shipment at no extra cost, subject to the review of the Village's security department.
